# Bob Smith

## biography

Bob Smith is a seasoned software engineer with over 15 years of experience in full-stack web development. Born and raised in Portland, Oregon, Bob discovered his passion for programming at a young age when he built his first website at 12.

After graduating from Oregon State University with a degree in Computer Science, Bob joined several startups where he honed his skills in building scalable web applications. He is known for his expertise in TypeScript, React, and Node.js.

Bob is an active contributor to open source projects and regularly speaks at tech conferences. When he's not coding, you can find him hiking the trails of the Pacific Northwest or experimenting with home-brewed coffee.
