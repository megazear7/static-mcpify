# Steve Baker

## biography

Steve Baker is a creative product designer and front-end developer who bridges the gap between beautiful design and functional code. Originally from Austin, Texas, Steve studied Interaction Design at the Savannah College of Art and Design.

With a decade of experience in the tech industry, Steve has worked with Fortune 500 companies and nimble startups alike. He specializes in design systems, accessibility, and creating delightful user experiences.

Steve is passionate about mentoring junior designers and developers. He runs a popular YouTube channel on UI/UX best practices and has authored two books on modern web design patterns. In his free time, Steve enjoys playing guitar and sketching cityscapes.
